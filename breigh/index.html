Bio

Breigh is a New York based singer-songwriter who uses the shimmering and energetic space of pop, combined with grounded and introspective lyrics to bring you into the duality that comes from the romantization and lived experiences of life. In her new single 'On The Table' She walks us through the do-or-die feelings that come with giving your all for a relationship only to be met with apathy. A painful moment yet you still can't help but to dance with the song.

The story

Breigh, pronounced like slay, built a growing fan based dubbed the 'Slayers' by first building a group chat of 600 dedicated fans, with over 75% active everyday. Intuitively creating this direct to fan method by focusing on what is most important to her, understanding what her fans enjoy about her art. Now with a very small team of independent music professionals she works hard to prove that the music industry is still based on good music, deep passion, and of course the music loving fans.
